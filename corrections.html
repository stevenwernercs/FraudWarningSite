Corrections and Clarifications

This page explains how visitors can request a correction, clarification, or context update when a source link, summary, or reference on the site appears inaccurate, stale, or materially incomplete.

A useful request should include:

  • The URL or page section at issue.
  • A short explanation of what is inaccurate or outdated.
  • Any public record, court order, correction, or other supporting material.
  • A reliable contact method for follow-up.
Contact: info@crooked.contractors

Good-faith requests should identify the specific page or claim at issue so the underlying source material can be reviewed and, when appropriate, corrected, clarified, or updated.
